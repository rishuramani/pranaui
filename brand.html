Accessibility Rules
Green as text: Use #18856a (400) on light backgrounds — passes AA at 4.5:1. Never use #2dd4a0 (300) as readable text on light backgrounds (fails at 1.9:1). Bright green is for decorative fills, borders, icons, and dark-mode text only.

Body text: Use #7a6348 (Driftwood) for muted body text — passes AA at 5.6:1. Use #2a1f10 (Espresso) for primary text — passes AAA at 15.9:1.

Dark mode: #2dd4a0 works as accent text on dark backgrounds (6.4:1 on #0d3d2e).

12 · System

Theme Modes

Light mode (Golden Sand) is the default across all surfaces. Dark mode is available for user preference and extended-use admin interfaces. Toggle the switch at the top right to preview.
Light Mode (Default)
Golden Sand warm neutrals — Linen background (#faf6f0), Parchment cards (#fffdf9), Sandstone borders (#e2d5c4). Deeper green accent (#18856a) for text. Communicates approachability, warmth, and trust. Used for marketing site, brand materials, client-facing dashboards, documentation, and onboarding.
Dark Mode (Supported)
Rich dark backgrounds (#0c0e0d), elevated cards (#141716), bright teal accent (#2dd4a0). Available as user preference across all admin interfaces. Partner dashboards may default to dark for extended use. Communicates technical depth and focus.
