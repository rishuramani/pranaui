FAQ
Common questions
What's a "partner seat"?+
A partner seat is one agency-level login — a team member at your agency who manages clients on PranaOS. Partner seats are separate from your clients: the per-seat fee covers your agency's access, while workspaces (billed at $50 each) cover every client and department you manage. A workspace is any client or department on PranaOS.
What are usage costs?+
Usage costs cover AI inference (LLM calls) and third-party API usage that agents make on behalf of your clients. You see these costs transparently in your dashboard and pass them through to clients with a markup. Most partners apply a 20% markup on usage as a baseline, but you can set whatever works for your business.
Can I white-label PranaOS?+
On Growth plans, you can customize branding per client (logos, colors). Full white-label — where PranaOS branding is completely removed and replaced with yours — is available on Enterprise plans. Your clients will never need to know what's under the hood.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?+
We'll give you a heads-up as you approach limits. Nothing breaks — we'll just ask you to upgrade or contact us. We never shut down your clients' access without warning.
Is there a money-back guarantee?+
Yes. If you cancel within your first 30 days, you get a full refund — no questions asked. We want you to commit with confidence, not risk.
How does billing work for my clients?+
PranaOS bills you directly for platform costs. You bill your clients whatever you want — we give you built-in billing tools (see the Client Billing screen) to set per-seat pricing, usage markup, custom line items, and generate invoices. Your margin is yours.
Can I build custom agents?+
On Growth and Enterprise plans, you can configure and customize marketplace agents extensively. Full custom agent development — building agents from scratch with custom logic, integrations, and GUIs — is available on Enterprise with our support team.
